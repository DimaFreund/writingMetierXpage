Writing Metier guarantees to deliver papers according to the initial instructions provided by the customer. However, you may request revision of the paper if the order does not satisfy the instructions. Alternatively, you may request a refund.

Keep in mind that requesting a refund requires a valid reason: double charge, late delivery, the absence of delivery, high level of plagiarism detection, or order cancelation.

Upon placing the order, upload as many files as necessary to guide your personal writer. Please note that uploading additional files after 30 minutes of the purchase will mark the files as additional details (unless you specify their inclusion earlier with Support Representative), which removes the responsibility from the writer to follow the newly uploaded files.

We strongly encourage you to provide specific features of your paper such as unique sources, speech patterns and clich√©s, unique terminology, and the specific structure of your paper. In case your assignment requires sources you do not have access to, please inform the Support Specialist.
Please note that ignoring specific features of your paper may lead to our conclusion to return 70% of the order price or use alternative sources for your paper.

To refund your order, please send a message to dispute@writingmetier.com with "Refund Request; Order#XXXXX"subject. In your message, you should list several valid arguments (a report from PlagScan and Copyscape indicating a high percentage of plagiarism, for example).

Upon receiving your refund request, it will take us from 7 to 25 business days to process the request. Once the dispute manager concludes your request, you will be notified via email.

If the writer delivered your work too late, you reserve the right to request a 20% refund of the initial price. Please note that particular deadlines are set to exceed no more than:

  • 20 min for 3 hours orders
  • 40 min for 8 hours orders
  • 60 min for 16 hours orders
  • 2 hours for 24 hours orders
  • 4 hours for 48 hours orders
  • 8 hours for 5 days orders
  • 16 hours for 14 days orders

Be aware that you can request a full refund only if your paper has not been delivered to you. Canceling the order after its delivery will not grant you a full refund.

If you previously placed an identical order or was charged twice, you can request a full refund too. We also guarantee to refund the full price of your order if the paper remains uncompleted.
